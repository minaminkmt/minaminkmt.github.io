About Me

Hello! I'm Minami Nakamata, a passionate digital artist with a strong drive for creating interactive and immersive experiences. I enjoy exploring the cute aesthetics through visuals and sonic features, and representing traditional art culture and values of my home country. This portfolio is a reflection of my journey, showcasing projects that highlight my skills and dedication.

In my free time, you can find me picking up a new hobby. My recent favorites are crochet, dining, and tennis. I'm always eager to learn and take on new challenges, so feel free to reach out!
