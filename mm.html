Melbourne Museum Project

is a project proposal for the Melbourne Museum, proposing the concept and prototype of an exhibition about mental health. My roles were group leader, creating the 3D model of the museum and the app UI design.

User journey based on user research. This was the foundation to build the exhibit and the in-app experience.
Writing out the functions of the app, breaking down the experience into key components and features.

App mockup developed based on the wireframe. Features that will be used in the museum.
In-app experience that the users can take away and use outside of the museum.
