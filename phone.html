GG-SITE is a creative coding project

A nostalgic 3D model of the LG env2 flip phone, referencing the iconic technology of the 2000s and a central object in our shared cultural history.
A small, witty pop-up notification appears on the phone screen, signaling the arrival of an exclusive message from the anonymous blogger, Gossip Girl.
The phone's inbox displays one new email, a link to the main blog post, inviting the user into the heart of the customized drama.
A full-screen view of the Gossip Girl blog, featuring a scrolling text post with the signature sharp wit and dramatic flair of the source material.
